Alaska

Alaska is a place that has been on our list for quite a while! In fact, the whole idea of Nordern Wanderers stemmed from our discussions about this trip. We realized that we want to document this experience under one name, and a name which we own. So far, the plan is to drive from our home city of Mississauga, Ontario, all the way up to Deadhorse, Alaska. We at Nordern Wanderers are merely waiting until both of us have graduated from University by 2023 and until enough funds been saved. We hope that by then, we will be able to travel beyond Canada's borders!
