Poisson-Blanc Lake, Quebec

This trip carried us up the mountainous region of Southern Quebec (also known as, The Laurentian Mountains) from Ottawa! In this excursion, the team, accompanied by our good friend, Santiago, summited the Poisson Blanc de la Montagne Du Fort. During the hike, we were met with muddy and slippery conditions due to recent rainfall and the leaves that have freshly fallen. Furthermore, the steep hills made the climb a little more technical. There's a reason that part of the hiking trails here were lined with ropes and not steps! But all in all, these conditions made summiting this mountain even more rewarding! We at Nordern Wanderers would definitely recommend it to anyone who is interested in doing an intermediate-level hike. Plus the parking is free!
