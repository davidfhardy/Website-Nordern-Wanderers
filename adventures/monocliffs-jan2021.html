Mono Cliffs Provincial Park, Ontario

Mono Cliffs provincial park offers adventure seekers a great place to hike around in any season! It runs along the Bruce trail and has some wonderful vantage points which lie on the Niagara escarpment. Along the hiking trail on the cliffs, there are a few platforms and stairs that descend about 30 metres which offer breathtaking views! Whether you are new to hiking or are a pro, you will enjoy what Mono Cliffs has to offer. We at Nordern Wanderers recommend a winter hike at this beautiful park. But, we are sure that it is equally beautiful in other seasons!
