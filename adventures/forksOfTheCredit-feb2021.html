Forks of the Credit Provincial Park

Forks of the Credit is one of those places where it's simply fun to drive to and through! The route to this park is indeed, quite scenic! You may pull over on the side of the road in case you want to get a better glimpse of the scenery. The Forks themselves are sprinkled with various rivers, creeks, and hiking trails. So you can easily spend an entire day exploring this area with all its nooks and crannies. Enjoy if you go! - David Hardy solo
