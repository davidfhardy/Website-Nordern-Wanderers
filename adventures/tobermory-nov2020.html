Tobermory, Ontario

Tobermory is hands-down one of the most beautiful places in Southern Ontario. The water here is so clear, it's basically crystal! The forests are vast and the hiking trails are full of wonderful sights. And after the Sun sets, the stars come out of hiding and dance all over the night sky!
