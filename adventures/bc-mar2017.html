British Columbia

It being my first time on the West coast of Canada, I truly had no idea what to expect. I've only ever read about and seen pictures of the great Canadian Rockies in textbooks and on social media. However, the moment the plane touched down and the road trip started, I was blown away. At the time, I was still an amateur in photography and this was my first big nature excursion! My two measly sized, 16gb SD cards could barely handle the amount of RAW pictures I was taking in a day during this trip! Wherever you looked, from the ride up the mountainous regions toward Whistler, the ferry ride to Vancouver Island, the road to the Pacific ocean, to the city life of BC, they all offered me great memories! The landscape there were some of the best I have ever seen; and since then, I have been itching to return! Some memories just happened to be captured by my old and cheap Canon DSLR at the time. Lucky for you! See some of them below! - Raphael Mabalot solo trip
