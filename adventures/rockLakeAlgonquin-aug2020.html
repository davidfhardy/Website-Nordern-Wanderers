Rock Lake, Algonquin Park, Ontario

Rock Lake is located within Ontario's second largest park, namely, Algonquin provincial park. It is just one of the 1500+ lakes located in this beauty of a park! The views along the trails in Rock Lake are absolutely stunning! Not to mention the lake itself - see our canoe photos below! We hiked Booth's Rock trail and the peek revealed a small part of the vast highlands in Algonquin park. Suffice it to say, it was beautiful up there! In this part of Algonquin park, you may camp (which we did) as well as rent canoes and PFD's which allow you to explore this beautiful area at an extended period of time and at a different perspective!
