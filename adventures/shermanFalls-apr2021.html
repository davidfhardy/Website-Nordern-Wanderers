Sherman & Borer's Falls, Hamilton, Ontario

If you're ever within the Hamilton area, Sherman Falls is a must-see! Its cascading waters feel very gentle, so you can truly get up close to the falls. Heck, you can probably shower in it if you wanted to (but that's probably prohibited and we do not condone or encourage this). The waterfall is also surrounded by an escarpment, giving you a more "engulfed by nature" feel, even though this place is only about 200 metres from the main road. And when you are done staring at the waterfall (in awe), you may cross the street and hike along the Bruce Trail. Two in one? Heck yeah!

The Borer's Falls is also in Hamilton. It is much larger than Sherman's Falls and as a result, it is more difficult to get a close-up view. Especially because there are no pathways to get to the bottom of the falls. But nonetheless, there are still some great views you can get of the raging waterfall. And the best part about this waterfall is that it is really easy to find. I didn't even need a map when I got there because I was able to hear the waterfall all the way at the park entrance (about 500 metres). - David Hardy solo trip
