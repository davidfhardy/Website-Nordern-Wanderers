We are the Nordern Wanderers

So far, we are two guys from Ontario, Canada who really like adventure. Our current ultimate adventure of sorts is that of a road trip beginning in our hometown up to the Polar Bears in Deadhorse, Alaska. Although vastly inexperienced and worse yet, tight on funds… our ambition to undertake such a journey merely grows, day by day.

Before we take on Alaska, we tackle all kinds of trips ranging from local to far (not super far – but for undergrads, they are). In doing so, we at Nordern Wanderers, test our limits, form a stronger bond, and yearn for more adventure.

Together, as Nordern Wanderers, we want to show people that exploration and adventure is truly the name of the game. Sure, it's nice to dream about your vacation, but living it beats that! Realize your adventure.
