ABOUT US

Nordern Wanderers

Who are the Nordern Wanderers? Awesome of you to ask! We are a team of passionate and driven adventurers who travel merely within our means. Our equipment, far from professional-grade and our knowledge in overlanding, humbly shallow, yet our yearn to explore stands ever strong.

Founded in the beginning of 2021 by Raphael Mabalot and David Hardy, we stand to show people that adventure is always within reach, and that, no matter how small, adventure is always worth realizing. Though currently, the team consists of only Raphael and David, we still strive to produce quality content whenever we can.

The current goal for Nordern Wanderers is a 14,000km road trip, heading North, up to Deadhorse, Alaska from their hometown in Mississauga and back. An arduous task for mere university students, but if our aim is to realize adventure, then how better to show it? Stick around! We have yet to showcase our best work.

Meet the Team

Raphael Mabalot

Hi there! Here’s a bit about myself without spoiling too much (I’ll save the rest in case you’d like to grab lunch someday). I’m currently a university student, studying IT at Carleton University. No, I’m not majoring in Overlanding but instead, in Optics and Photonics – much more boring, I know. I was born in the Philippines where I grew up for a while until my family and I moved to Canada. Which is where I now reside, study and work. The true north, strong and free.

So, why did I decide to start this brand with David…? A broke university student with a vastly different academic gearing… I believe, the reason stems from my love for photography and videography. Whenever I travel, my camera always stays strapped on me and the buffer, always filled.

I plan to use Nordern Wanderers as a platform to grow my passion for photo and video and to feed my hunger for adventure and exploration. Oh yeah, need I mention? I LOVE TO TRAVEL. No matter how near or far the destination… heck, no destination. I’ll drive, hike, canoe, whatever the case – I'll just go! After my homework of course.

David Hardy

Hello! I am the other half of the Nordern Wanderers team. I’m currently 21 years old and I live in Ontario, Canada. When I’m not out photographing for Nordern Wanderers, I am either working or studying Electrical Engineering at the University of Waterloo.

Why did I form this brand with Raphael? Well, for starters, I just enjoy exploring new places. But the main reason is because I wanted some sort of medium to document my adventures, and Nordern Wanderers is a great way to fill that gap.

I will use Nordern Wanderers to continue adventuring to new places, enhance my photography, and share my experiences with all of you.
